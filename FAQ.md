# AppleJr FAQ

## What is AppleJr?

AppleJr is an iOS guide website for IPA installation, ESign, Scarlet, KSign, DNS profile, and sideloading tutorials.

## Can I install IPA files on iPhone without PC?

Yes, you can use iOS signing apps such as ESign, Scarlet, or KSign.

## Why does my iPhone say Unable to Verify App?

This usually happens when the developer profile, certificate, or app verification is not working properly.

## Why does my iPhone say Unable to Install App?

This can happen because of revoked certificates, blocked installation, unsupported iOS version, or a broken IPA file.
